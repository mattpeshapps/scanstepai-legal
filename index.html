Privacy Policy for ScanStep AI

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

Summary: ScanStep AI processes your photos using third-party AI services to generate 3D CAD files. All generated files are stored locally on your device. We do not create user accounts, track your activity, or sell your data.

1. Introduction

ScanStep AI ("the App") is developed by Matt Peschisolido ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using ScanStep AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2a. Photos and Camera Data

The App accesses your device camera and photo library with your permission. You provide photos of mechanical parts for 3D model generation. These photos are:

  • Sent to Google Gemini (Google AI) for dimensional analysis and feature extraction
  • Processed to extract measurement data and part geometry

Photos are transmitted directly from your device to Google's servers for processing. We do not store your photos on any server we operate. Google's Gemini API does not use your submitted data to train its models. Data sent via the API is processed for your request only and is not retained by Google for model improvement purposes, in accordance with Google's API terms.

2b. Part Descriptions

You may enter text descriptions of your parts (materials, features, dimensions). This text is sent to Google Gemini along with your photos and to Zoo.dev for CAD generation.

2c. CAD Generation Data

Extracted part analysis data (dimensions, features, geometry — not your original photos) is sent to Zoo.dev (KittyCAD) to generate STEP and 3D model files. The API processes your request and returns the generated file. Zoo.dev does not use your data to train AI models.

2d. Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store via StoreKit. We do not collect or store your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. Your token balance is stored locally on your device.

2e. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require user accounts or login
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or personal identifiers
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • We do not collect device identifiers or location data
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used solely to provide the App's core functionality:

  • Photos → Google Gemini: Analyzed for part dimensions, features, and geometry
  • Analysis data → Zoo.dev: Used to generate precision STEP/CAD files
  • Token balance: Tracked locally to manage your generation credits
  • Generated files: Stored locally on your device in the App's Documents directory

4. Third-Party Services

ScanStep AI uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Google Gemini (Google AI) — Processes photos for dimensional analysis. Data is not used for model training. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Zoo.dev (KittyCAD) — Generates 3D CAD models from analyzed data. Subject to Zoo.dev's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — Handles in-app purchases. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not control how these third parties handle data once it is transmitted to their servers. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All generated STEP files, 3D previews, thumbnails, and part metadata are stored locally on your device
  • API keys you enter in the App's Settings are stored locally on your device
  • Token balances are stored locally using iOS standard storage mechanisms
  • We do not operate any backend servers or databases that store your data

Data transmitted to third-party APIs (Google Gemini, Zoo.dev) uses encrypted HTTPS connections.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete individual parts from the App's library at any time
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data
  • Photos and data sent to Google Gemini via the API are not retained by Google for model training and are subject to Google's standard data retention policies
  • Data sent to Zoo.dev is subject to their respective retention policies

7. Children's Privacy

ScanStep AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is designed for mechanical engineers, R&D technicians, and adult hobbyists.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store personal information on our servers, there is no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete. All App data is stored locally on your device and is under your direct control.

For data sent to third-party services (Google, Zoo.dev), please refer to their privacy policies for information about exercising your data rights.
